Use justify-content again to help these frogs get to their lilypads. Remember that this CSS-Bootstrap property aligns items horizontally and accepts the following values:
  • justify-content-start: Items align to the left side of the container.
  • justify-content-end: Items align to the right side of the container.
  • justify-content-center: Items align at the center of the container.
  • justify-content-between: Items display with equal spacing between them.
  • justify-content-around: Items display with equal spacing around them.
